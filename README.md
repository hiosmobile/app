<h2>Introduction</h2>
<p>Welcome to the official source code repository for HiOS, the dedicated application from The Highland Cafe™ Enterprises. This modern version has been completely rebuilt from the ground up using React and Capacitor to deliver a faster, more fluid, and highly customizable native experience.</p>

<h2>About HiOS</h2>
<p>HiOS serves as the central platform for accessing the diverse services offered by The Highland Cafe™ Enterprises. This application streamlines your interaction with our brands, allowing you to:</p>
<ul>
  <li>Browse menus and order from <strong>HiCafe™</strong>, <strong>Cafe Fiesta</strong>, and our <strong>Breakfast</strong> services.</li>
  <li>Manage room bookings, explore <strong>Hotel Activities</strong>, and utilize digital room keys at our <strong>weB&B</strong> properties.</li>
  <li>Track your points and redeem exclusive offers through the all-new <strong>HiRewards</strong> dashboard.</li>
  <li>Fully customize your app experience with dynamic themes, custom wallpapers, and our signature <strong>ZenGlass</strong> UI.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Android Installation Guide</h2>
<p>To install the native HiOS app on your Android device, please follow these steps:</p>
<ol>
  <li><strong>Download the Latest APK:</strong> Open a web browser on your Android device and navigate to our <a href="https://github.com/hiosmobile/app/releases/latest">latest releases page</a> to download the most recent <code>.apk</code> file.</li>
  <li><strong>Access Downloaded File:</strong> Open your device's file management application (such as the built-in "Files" app or Google Files, available on the <a href="https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.google.android.apps.nbu.files">Google Play Store</a> if not installed). Locate the <strong>Downloads</strong> folder and tap the downloaded <code>.apk</code> file.</li>
  <li><strong>Enable Unknown Sources (if prompted):</strong> If you haven't previously installed applications from outside the Google Play Store, your device may prompt you to allow installation from "Unknown Apps." Follow the on-screen instructions to enable this permission for your file management application.</li>
  <li><strong>Initiate Installation:</strong> Once the necessary permissions are granted, the system will ask if you wish to install or update HiOS. Tap <strong>Install</strong> to proceed.</li>
  <li><strong>Enjoy HiOS:</strong> Upon successful installation, you can find the HiOS icon in your app drawer and begin using the application.</li>
</ol>

<h2>iOS Installation Guide (Progressive Web App - PWA)</h2>
<p>HiOS is also accessible on iOS and any other device as a Progressive Web App (PWA):</p>
<ol>
  <li><strong>Visit HiOS on the web:</strong> Open Safari on your iOS device and go to <a href="https://hiosmobile.github.io/app">HiOS on web</a>.</li>
  <li><strong>Add to Home Screen:</strong> Tap the <strong>Share</strong> icon (usually a square with an arrow pointing upwards). In the share sheet, scroll down and select <strong>Add to Home Screen</strong>.</li>
  <li><strong>Open HiOS Lite:</strong> A HiOS icon will be added to your home screen. Tap this icon to launch the application.</li>
</ol>

<h2>Updating HiOS on iOS (PWA)</h2>
<p>To ensure you have the latest version of HiOS Lite on iOS:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Open the <strong>Settings</strong> app.</li>
  <li>Navigate to <strong>Safari</strong>.</li>
  <li>Select <strong>Advanced</strong> > <strong>Website Data</strong> (or <strong>Cookies and Saved Site Data</strong> depending on your iOS version).</li>
  <li>Search for and select <strong>thehighlandcafe.github.io</strong>.</li>
  <li>Tap <strong>Remove All Website Data</strong>. This action will clear the cached data and ensure the next time you open HiOS, it fetches the latest version.</li>
</ol>
<p><em>You can also install this web version on Android, although it is recommended that you go for installing the full native Android APK for the best, most native and fast experience.</em></p>

<p><strong>Please Note:</strong> An active internet connection is required to access all features and services within HiOS.</p>
